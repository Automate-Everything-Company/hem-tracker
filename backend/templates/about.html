About Hemophilia Tracker

Hemophilia Tracker is designed to help individuals with hemophilia gain more insight regarding their treatment.
By providing an easy-to-use interface, users can input their blood work data and visualize their current factor level and also over the week.
This is not a medical tool, please consult your doctor before making any decisions.
Read our disclaimer.

Purpose

Our mission is to provide a free, reliable tool that supports hemophilia patients in understanding their condition better, thereby improving their quality of life.
Hemophilia Tracker aims to bridge the gap between clinical treatment and day-to-day management.

How It Works

  1. Input your peak factor level after an infusion.
  2. Record the time of the infusion and the time of the subsequent blood sample draw.
  3. Our tool calculates the decay constant and generates a plot showing the factor level over time.

Features

  • Interactive Playground: Experiment with predefined values to see how factor levels change over time.
  • Custom Measurements: Register and for a customized analysis and monitoring.
  • Real-Time Plots: Visualize your factor levels and their decay in real-time in your users area.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to navigate and input your data.

Benefits

By using Hemophilia Tracker, you can:

  • Understand the efficacy of your treatment better.
  • Make informed decisions about your health and treatment plan.
  • Track your condition more accurately and consistently.

We hope that Hemophilia Tracker will become a valuable tool in your health management toolkit, providing you with insights and confidence in managing hemophilia.
